About Oxygenate

Oxygenate is an online store which allows the shopper to buy plants
that are specifically grown for filtering the air. It would be a small
business run from the hamilton region and expand to auckland and the
southern waikato region. As it is a small intimate business the client has
said they wouldn/t want to expand the delivery region for now as they
would be delivering the plants themselves. This is to reduce stress on the
plant that could occur if it had been couriered or rough handled.
