Plant Care

All you need to know about how to care for your plants.

  • Sunlight light direction
    South: Provides the most light.
    East: Light drops off quickly.
    West: Same light conditions as
    eastward facing windows
    but accompanied by more heat.
    North: Provides the lowest light.

  • Water
    Check soil dampness with finger,
    water if first inch is dry.
    Gently lift foliage, flood pot and
    leave for 30 minutes. Drain excess.
    Check plant every 3-4 days to determine
    if it needs watering.

  • Humidity & Temperature
    Plants prefer similar temperature as humans.
    Avoid placing near vents or other temperature hazards.

  • Nutrients & Fertalising
    Plants get their nutrients from the water,
    sun and potting mix.
    Adding fertaliser is optional but acceptable.
